Distance from Home

Places of publication are plotted on a map of Britain. Playback again steps chronologically through the years, one tone per year — but now the volume of each tone falls away with how far that year's average place of publication sat from a reference point you choose. Pitch still follows the number of items or pages, as on the timeline.
